Mission

Our project consists of two missions:
  • Primary mission - it's obligatory, its purpose is to measure the outside temperature and humidity during the flight. An important task of the probe is also to record telemetry on the built-in memory card and transmit data to the ground station via radio.
  • Secondary mission - VarSat1 has advanced systems, which can be used in secondary mission. The probe will largely focus on measurments of the atmosphere and flight trajectory - on board there will be, among others: humidity, acceleration and rotation sensors, GPS position and altitude, a magnetic field sensor, and advanced radio communication module, a voltage sensors and a fast storage memory. An important factor is the ability to measure atmospheric pollution (AQI), COâ‚‚ concentration, and the content of volatile organic compounds in the air. Our goal is also to analyze this data - based on the results it will be possible to determine the quality of the atmosphere and the chance of life on a planet.


Technical specifications of VarSat 1


Hardware:

UVCS-alpha:
  • Main chip: ESP32-D0WDQ6
  • Temperature, humidity and pressure sensor: BME280
  • Accelerometer, gyroscope: MPU6050
  • GPS module: Neo-6m
  • Radio: HC-12 433MHz 100mW
  • Onboard Micro SD memory card
  • Battery voltage sensor, status LEDs, speaker
  • Other chip-integrated sensors and free GPIO for future expansion

Ground station:
  • Main board:
    Arduino UNO AT328P
  • Radio: HC-12 433MHz 100mW
  • Yagi directional antenna
Firmware and software:
  • ASTCF - advanced satellite telemetry and control firmware. Based on RTOS and C++ programs in the Arduino IDE enviroment. Reliable, efficient and versatile.
  • VSRMS - versatile satellite remote management software. Based on Python and common GUI libraries. Modern look and access to all satellite options.


Flight plan


Rocket carries the satellite to the height of ~1.5km.

In the rocket flight apogee, satellites are dropped.

Satellite measures all sorts of data, while descending on a parachute.

Good parachute design allows for safe landing.
