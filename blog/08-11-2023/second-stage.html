First successes - announcement of elimination results

08.11.2023r.



We are honored to annouce, that our project has achieved an important success. As one of 33 teams from Poland we have qualified for the second stage of the CanSat 2023/2024 competition! Thank you very much for Your support! Without it, we wouldn't be able to celebrate such an important moment. This motivates us to even more intensive work on satellite.

We would also like to congratulate the other teams that made it to the second stage. Even though we fight for the next places together, the ability to compete at such a level is amazing.

Nex goal? February 2024. This is the deadline for submitting the second documentation - CDR (Critical Design Review).

We should also receive CanSat Kits from the Copernicus Science Center - thank you very much for sending them.

Once again, we would like to thank you very, very much ❤️


Best,
VarSat Team
