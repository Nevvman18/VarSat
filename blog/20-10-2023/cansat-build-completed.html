CanSat build almost complete!

20.10.2023r.



We would like to inform you that after 2 weeks of hard work, we have almost finished working on the satellite prototype! We have already tested it at home and everything works perfectly. We are still waiting for the purchase of an atmosphere composition sensor and we need to make a parachute.
However, we have completed the on-board computer:
We have also printed the enclousre on a 3D printer:
As always, the building process had some problems. The tight fit of the elements required us to slightly grind the mounting rails, and we have to redesign the housing itself - the speaker hole should have a different pattern, because the printer isn't good at printing narrow gaps.
For the end, a few more words. Mateusz and Wiktor started the base station program and we already have a partially working version. Kuba and Franek are writing the first PDR documentation. The FSDM Foundation has approved the funding application and will add the article to their website within a few days.

The work is still going on, because the deadline for submitting the PDR is October 23, and on November 6 we will find out whether we have qualified to the next part. Keep your fingers crossed, we will keep you updated on the status of work on the satellite.
